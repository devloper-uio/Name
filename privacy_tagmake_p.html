Privacy Policy

TagMake Pro is an AI-powered YouTube tool developed to enhance content discovery, analysis, and keyword optimization. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information Collected

  • No personal user account or registration is required.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • YouTube video URLs are processed only temporarily for analysis and are not stored.
  • No cookies or local user tracking is used.

APIs and Third-Party Services

This application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google YouTube Data API v3 – to fetch public video/channel statistics and metadata.
  • No API keys or tokens from users are requested or stored.

Advertising Partners

TagMake Pro uses third-party advertising services to support our app:

  • StartApp Ads – May collect anonymous device data such as IP address, device ID, and app usage patterns to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Unity Ads – May access advertising identifiers and analytics to deliver in-app advertisements and measure engagement.

These services may use tracking technologies such as SDKs or cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored by us.

Security

All requests are processed over HTTPS to maintain secure communication. We do not store any user data or video history.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 13.
